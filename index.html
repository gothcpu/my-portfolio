Hi there! I'm Lucius, as the giant letters above say.

I'm a Metro-Detroit native currently based in New York City. Before stumbling into STEM, I found my passion in Sociology and cultural analysis in hopes of better understanding patterns in our society. Taking a required Intro to Python course in college completely rocked my world, and allowed me to combine my passion for understanding others with the ability to create things that can make people's lives easier.

Fast forwarding to now, I'm a second year Computer Science student with a 4.0 GPA and residing member of PTK Honor Society, however I'm currently on leave due to the pandemic. In search of more immediate workforce experience, I ended up a Flatiron School graduate with an even further expanded tech toolkit.
